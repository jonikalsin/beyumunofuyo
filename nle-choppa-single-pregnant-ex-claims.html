NLE Choppa Insists Hes Single Despite Pregnant Exs Claims

April 25, 2024
blog

NLE Choppa has doubled down on his claims that he’s single, despite his pregnant ex-girlfriend’s claims to the contrary.

The Memphis rapper appeared on the Atlanta-based radio show The Morning Hustle on Thursday (April 20), where he addressed the ongoing drama with ex Marissa DeNae. While he did confirm her claims that she’s expecting their child as well as the fact he paid for her IVF treatments, he insisted he was still very much a single man.

“I’m not in a relationship,” Choppa told host Lore’l. “Some people delusional. I am a free man. I am God’s creation. Don’t nobody own me. I ain’t got no cell block number. [Yeah, I paid for] IVF. I think she worded it wrong. I went through IVF because we previously had a miscarriage and that was pretty much my way of another chance at it in a sense.”

He continued: “But it was understood from the jump that I wasn’t in a relationship with no one. I just felt like – I don’t know. I ain’t never owed nobody nothing but I just felt kind of sorry if that makes sense. So I just wanted to try to make that situation right. And I’m still the asshole.”

The interview arrived just days after Marissa took to social media to publicly shame Choppa for not being there for her and their future child as he’s allegedly skipped several recent doctor appointments.

“I Really been trying to hold it down and keep it together,” she wrote in an Instagram Story. “But I’m honestly just fed tf up. You constantly trying to holla at folks, take folks on dates and etc but you have failed to show up for your child!

“That YOU PAID FOR ME TO HAVE! You think because you may pay a doctor bill or send a few dollars for certain shit that changes the fact that you’re absent as fuck. I’ve had 31 appointments since we’ve placed our child in my stomach and you haven’t shown up to one.”

She continued: “You’re telling me, you don’t got no time for doctor’s appointments but you got time to wanna take folks on vegan dates? You got time to wanna continue to shoot your shot but not for your kid?? You got time to wanna fall in love with someone else.”
