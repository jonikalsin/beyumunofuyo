A Mysterious Component

The Mysterious Component (Japanese: ふしぎなパーツ Mysterious Parts) is an item in Pokémon GO. Since November 2019, Team GO Rocket Grunts will drop a Mysterious Component upon being defeated. If the player collects six Mysterious Components, they will combine into a Rocket Radar, which can be used to locate and battle Team GO Rocket Leaders.

Professor Willow hypothesized that Mysterious Components are what Team GO Rocket uses to invade PokéStops; however, they appear to have other functions as well.[1] Following the end of GO Fest 2020, a video was posted on Twitter showing the Team GO Rocket Leaders each using a Mysterious Component to communicate with Giovanni.[2]

In Pokémon GO

Effect

If the player has collected six Mysterious Components, they will automatically be combined into a Rocket Radar.

Acquisition

One Mysterious Component is awarded upon defeating a Team GO Rocket Grunt. If a player already has at least one Rocket Radar on hand, no more Mysterious Components will be awarded until all Rocket Radars are used up. However, Mysterious Components can still be obtained via Research tasks.
