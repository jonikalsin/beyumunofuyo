The Top 10 Highest Paid Coaches in the NBA 2024

July 15, 2024

The NBA has seen a surge in the importance and value of head coaches in recent years. The highest-paid coaches are recognized for their significant impact on their teams' success and the development of players. Here's a comprehensive overview of the top 10 highest-paid NBA coaches for the 2023-24 season based on available information.

1. Gregg Popovich

  • Average Annual Salary: $16 million
  • Seasons with Team: 28
  • Team: San Antonio Spurs

Gregg Popovich, known for his impressive track record, has been coaching the Spurs since 1996. With five NBA titles and three NBA Coach of the Year awards, he has earned the most wins in NBA coaching history. His current five-year, $80 million contract extension solidifies his status as the highest-paid NBA coach.

2. Monty Williams

  • Average Annual Salary: $13.05 million
  • Team: Detroit Pistons

Despite the Phoenix Suns' struggles in the NBA Playoffs, Monty Williams commands the second-highest coaching salary. With a reputation built on successful stints as an assistant and head coach, there is mounting pressure on Williams to revive the fortunes of the Detroit Pistons.

3. Steve Kerr

  • Average Annual Salary: $9.5 million
  • Seasons with Team: 10
  • Team: Golden State Warriors

Steve Kerr, the head coach of the Golden State Warriors, has a successful track record, guiding the team to four NBA championship victories in the last nine years. His impact on the team's success has positioned him as one of the highest-paid coaches in the league.

4. Erik Spoelstra

  • Average Annual Salary: $8.5 million
  • Seasons with Team: 16
  • Team: Miami Heat

Erik Spoelstra recently received a record-breaking contract extension, making him one of the top-paid coaches in the NBA. With six NBA Finals appearances and two NBA titles with the Heat, Spoelstra's impact on the team's success is undeniable.

5. Nick Nurse

  • Average Annual Salary: $8 million
  • Team: Philadelphia 76ers

Nick Nurse, known for his coaching success, secured a high-paying contract with the Philadelphia 76ers. His proven track record and success with the Toronto Raptors have contributed to his placement among the highest-paid NBA coaches.

6. Jason Kidd

  • Team: Dallas Mavericks

Despite a challenging tenure with the Milwaukee Bucks and the underperformance of the Mavericks, Jason Kidd remains one of the highest-paid NBA coaches. His coaching potential and previous successes have contributed to his significant annual earnings.

7. Michael Malone

  • Team: Denver Nuggets

Michael Malone's much-deserved contract extension with the Denver Nuggets has made him one of the highest-paid coaches in the league. With aspirations of making the Nuggets a dynasty, Malone's impact on the team's performance is reflected in his substantial earnings.

8. Quin Snyder

  • Team: Atlanta Hawks

Quin Snyder, the head coach of the Atlanta Hawks, commands a significant salary, reflecting his importance to the team and his impact on player development and team performance.

9. Billy Donovan

  • Team: Chicago Bulls

Billy Donovan's role as the head coach of the Chicago Bulls has positioned him among the highest-paid coaches in the NBA. His coaching abilities and impact on the team's success are reflected in his substantial annual earnings.

10. Rick Carlisle

  • Team: Indiana Pacers

Rick Carlisle's coaching expertise and impact on the Indiana Pacers have secured him a place among the top 10 highest-paid NBA coaches for the 2023-24 season. His significant annual earnings reflect his value to the team and the league.
