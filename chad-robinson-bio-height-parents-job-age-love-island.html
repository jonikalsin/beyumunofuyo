Chad Robinson On Love Island

Who Are Chad Robinson Parents?

Chad Robinson’s parents are Antonio Robinson (age 60) and Toni King Robinson (age 60).

According to Tony’s Twitter handle, she is a kindergarten teacher by profession.

Chad’s parents have been married since 1986 and they celebrated their 35th wedding anniversary on July 21, 2021.

On their 35th anniversary, Chad’s parents wore cool t-shirts with the back of their t-shirt reading “Married Since 1986.” Chad’s mother took to Facebook to share that picture and captioned the post, “Thank you, thank you for all the anniversary love!! We are so blessed to have such supportive family and friends during these 35 years.”

Chad’s dad celebrates his birthday on the 4th of March every year. On March 4, 2019, Toni wished her happiest birthday to her husband, caregiver, father, a friend. She wrote, “Happy Birthday to the best husband, caregiver, father, friend…just the best! You truly have taken care of me during this crazy time in our life and I don’t take it for granted,” on Facebook.

Chad Robinson Job

According to Chad’s profile on the Peacock website, he is a personal trainer and a bartender by profession. He works at Chad Robinson Health and Fitness. His services include Personal Training, Health and Wellness Coaching, and Nutrition Planning.

As for Chad, the number one thing that makes his business stand out is his true passion for every aspect of overall health and fitness. Chad considers himself a very detail-oriented person and promises to cater to all needs of his clients. As a personal trainer, he will push his clients toward their health and fitness goals.

Love Island star’s LinkedIn handle shows us that he worked as an Engineering Intern at TPI Composites, Inc. from April 2020 to September 2020. He was a member of the nondestructive testing research team there. His responsibility there was to scan, test, apply ultrasonic, eddy current, and thermal testing to the composite products.

Chad went to Clarkson High School for education. For his undergraduate studies, he studied at Western Michigan University, from where he has a Bachelor of Arts degree in Communication.
