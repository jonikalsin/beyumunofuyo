Table of Biography

Florence Welch is an English singer, musician, model, designer, artist, and activist. Florence Welch has starred as the lead vocalist and songwriter of the indie rock band Florence and the Machine with a powerful Mezzo-Soprano voice. She can be heard on popular songs such as “Dogs Days Are Over” and “Shake It Out”.

Early Life

Florence Welch was born on 28 August 1986 and her current age is 36 years old as of today’s date. She was born in Camber well, London. She lived with her parents, father Nick Welch, and mother Evelyn Welch who later divorced when she was 13. Moreover, She also had two siblings, Grace Welch and J.J. Welch, and four step-siblings, Maddie, Sam, John, and Nick.

Her birth name was Florence Leontine Mary Welch along with nicknames like Fossy, Flo, and Floaty. She has both British and American citizenship. She is believed to be an agnostic and belongs to the Caucasian White ethnicity.

As for her education, she attended Thomas’s London Day School and then later went to Alleyn’s School. She later enrolled in Camberwell College of Arts but could not continue due to her pursuits in music.

Florence Welch – Professional life

Florence started her music pursuits with the formation of her band Florence and the Machine who first debuted with the album Lungs in July 2009, which created quite a buzz. This became famous among the fans with its hits of Dogs Days Are Over, Kiss With a Fist.

This made her popular across the Atlantic in the United States of America. She and her band have given spectacular hits amassed in albums like Ceremonials (2011), How Big, How Blue, How Beautiful (2015), and High as Hope (2018). She has also imprinted her footprints as an artist through her work in Sweet Nothing in 2012 and Wild Season in 2017.

Florence Welch – Brand Endorsements

Famous British singer Florence has also signed endorsement deals with some top brands. She has been selected as the brand ambassador for the world-famous Gucci watches and fine jewelry collection.
