Mark Ronson also known as Mark Daniel Ronson is a British-American DJ, songwriter, record producer and record executive. As of 2022, Mark Ronson’s net worth is $20 million. He is known for portraying Agency Control Room Technician in the animation, action, Adventure film “Spies in Disguise” (2019).

He is also well known for producing songs such Nothing Breaks Like a Heart (2019) and Uptown Funk (2015). He is the recipient of the Academy Award for Best Music (2019) and Grammy Award for Record of the Year (2008-2016).

Mark Ronson Wiki/Biography

Born on 4 September 1975, Mark Ronson’s age is 47 Years Old as of 2022. He was born in a well-settled family from St John’s Wood, London, United Kingdom. He holds a British nationality and has his belief in Jewish religion. His zodiac sign is Virgo.
