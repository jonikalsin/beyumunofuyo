Troy Smith Celebrity Golf Scramble to Support Ohio State University Athletes

May 23, 2024
blog

The highly anticipated Troy Smith Celebrity Golf Scramble is set to take place on April 12th at The Golf Club at Little Turtle in Westerville, Ohio. This event is aimed at supporting Ohio State University athletes and promises to be a star-studded affair featuring several Ohio State football legends and other notable athletes, including Braxton Miller and Beanie Wells. The tournament is a wonderful opportunity for fans and supporters to come together and celebrate the incredible career of Troy Smith at Ohio State University.

The Venue and Date

  • Location: The Golf Club at Little Turtle in Westerville, Ohio
  • Date: April 12th, 2024

Supporting Ohio State University Athletes

The Troy Smith Celebrity Golf Scramble is dedicated to supporting NIL (Name, Image, and Likeness) opportunities for Ohio State University athletes. With the participation of various Ohio State football legends and notable athletes, the event aims to raise awareness and support for the next generation of Ohio State athletes.

About Troy Smith

Troy Smith is one of the most decorated Ohio State football players of all time. A Heisman Trophy winner in 2006, he has been recognized as an All-American and has had an illustrious career in both college football and the NFL. The event serves as a tribute to his remarkable achievements and contributions to Ohio State University.

Event Details

  • Event Timing:
    • 8:00 AM - Registration Opens
    • 9:00 AM - Warm Up / Breakfast / Putting & Chipping Contests
    • 10:45 AM - Introduction & Call to Carts
    • 11:00 AM - Scramble Begins
    • 12:00 PM - Lunch served on course
    • 4:30 PM - Award Ceremony and Live Auction
    • 5:00 PM - Post Event Happy Hour

For additional details and registration, interested individuals can visit the official website of the event.

The Troy Smith Celebrity Golf Scramble is an excellent opportunity for fans to not only enjoy a day of golf but also to show their support for Ohio State University athletes. It promises to be a day filled with camaraderie, sportsmanship, and the celebration of an incredible career that has left an indelible mark on Ohio State University's football legacy.
