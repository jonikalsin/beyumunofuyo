Emmanuel Acho is an American television personality and former sportsperson who rose to fame as a linebacker in the past, having played in the National Football League. He was a 6th round draft pick of the football team Cleveland Browns in 2012 and got traded to the Philadelphia Eagles in 2013 where he continued playing until retiring from the sport in 2015. Emmanuel has worked as an analyst for the TV channel Fox Sports 1 and has anchored the weekly activist webcast Uncomfortable Conversations with a Black Man (2020). He has appeared in numerous other programs on the small screen including Get Up (2019-2020) where he was the analyst, Good Morning America Weekend Edition (2020) in which he was featured as a guest, and Acho’s Nachos (2014) in which he acted.

Education

He went to St. Mark’s School of Texas in Dallas, Texas, where he lettered in football, basketball, as well as track and field. As a senior, he was labeled the state’s top linebacker by Dave Campbell’s Texas Football and was declared to be one of the 150 top recruits in the country by the sports TV channel ESPNU.

He then attended the University of Texas, graduating with a Masters in Sports Psychology.
