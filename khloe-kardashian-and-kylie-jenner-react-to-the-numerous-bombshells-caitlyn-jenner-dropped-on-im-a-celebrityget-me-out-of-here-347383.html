Caitlyn Jenner has been doing a lot of talking while on British reality series, I’m a Celebrity…Get Me Out of Here!, and some members of the Kardashian/Jenner clan are not thrilled about what is being said.

The Keeping Up with the Kardashians star dropped the bombshell that she has not spoken to Khloe Kardashian for many years.

The former athlete also hinted that Kylie Jenner might be pregnant with her second baby. According to Caitlyn, Kendall Jenner was willing to drop everything to be Prince Harry’s wife.

A source spoke to TMZ and explained that Khloe is disappointed by Caitlyn’s words because she has been very present for her former stepfather.

According to Hollywood Life, Kylie and Kris Jenner are taking the revelation like this: “If anything, [Kylie] kind of just laughed at it. That’s just who her dad is in those kinds of situations. Kylie and Caitlyn have always had a really good relationship despite anyone else in the family’s feelings on Caitlyn. Kylie loves her dad very much and has stayed out of the drama with the Jenners and her mom, Kris, to just focus on her own relationship with her dad.”

Kylie, her mother, and the rest of the family watched Caitlyn when she participated in another version of the show, and they knew what to expect.

The pal revealed: “The family was made aware that she was going to do the show, and Caitlyn promised she wasn’t going to drag the family down in any way while she is a part of the show. The girls and Kris are very supportive of Caitlyn to do the show, she is getting paid amazingly well, and people are going to see the real person that Caitlyn is, and they fully support all of that.”

The person stated: “What people don’t realize is that Bruce did the U.S. version of the show, and now that Caitlyn is living her true life, she wants to have this experience as the real her, and the Kardashians and Kris are all for it. They are actually proud of her and look forward to seeing how she does and what she does; it should be pretty fun.”
